Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2025

Overview

HustleBot ("we," "our," or "us") is a Chrome extension designed to help resellers on Facebook Marketplace. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and your rights.

What Data We Access

HustleBot accesses the following data only while you are actively using the extension on Facebook Marketplace:

  • Page content — the title, price, and description text of the Facebook Marketplace listing you are currently viewing
  • Active tab URL — to determine whether you are on a Facebook Marketplace page

We do not access your Facebook account credentials, messages, friends list, personal profile, or any other account data.

How We Use Your Data

The listing data (title, price, description) is sent to our backend analysis service solely to generate a deal verdict, estimated profit, and ROI score. This data is:

  • Used only to generate your real-time deal analysis
  • Not stored permanently on our servers
  • Not sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties
  • Not used for advertising or profiling

Data Storage

HustleBot does not store personal information. Listing data sent for analysis is processed in real time and discarded immediately after your result is returned. We do not maintain databases of user activity or listing history.

Permissions We Request

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — to read the content of the Facebook Marketplace page you are currently viewing
  • scripting — to inject the content script that extracts listing data from the page

These permissions are the minimum necessary for the extension to function. We do not request access to your browsing history, bookmarks, or any other tabs.

Third-Party Services

HustleBot connects to our own backend analysis API. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services within the extension.

Children's Privacy

HustleBot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hustlebotai.com.
